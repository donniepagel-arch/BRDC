Triples DRAFT

How it works!

Teams consist of 3 players, each assigned a level of ‘A’ ‘B’ or ‘C’ based on their draft order.
Nights will consist of 9 sets worth 1 point each, 6 singles and 3 doubles.

Singles sets are best of 3, 501FI/Cricket/Choice and played against the player of the same level on the opposing team(‘A’v.‘A’, ‘B’v.‘B’, ‘C’v.‘C’).  Players will cork to start each leg with the player closest to the bull getting the start and, if necessary,  get the choice and start of 501FI or Cricket in the 3RD leg.

Doubles sets are best of 3. Each leg, teams will each choose a player to cork, with the closest to the bull getting to start. Teams can decide their player order each leg.

Set wins will result in 1 point each.  Teams will be awarded 1 point.

In the occasion that a player is unable to make it, any player of comparable ability can play in their place.  If interested in being an alternate please click here to sign up for free.

This is a Dartconnect managed league and will require a free account.  Sign up today!  dartconnect.com
