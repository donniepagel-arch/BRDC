# BRDC Feature Ideas & Wishlist

**Date:** January 21, 2026
**Status:** Ideas for future development

---

## Recently Completed

### Virtual Darts Game
- Browser-based dart training game
- 5 development phases planned
- Swipe-to-throw mechanics
- Practice mode with calibration
- See: `BRDC_Virtual_Darts_Master_Plan_v2.md`

### Dart Trader Marketplace
- Members can list dart equipment for sale
- Categories: Darts, Flights, Shafts, Cases, Dartboards, Accessories
- Darts have extra fields: weight, material, point type, brand
- Go-between model (no payment handling)
- Filter by category, price, condition

### Community Events Map
- Interactive map with event markers
- Event types: Tournament, League Night, Blind Draw, Social, Other
- Dart type filter: Steel Tip, Soft Tip, Both
- Recurring events: Weekly, Bi-weekly, Monthly
- Host/organizer info with logo and website
- Anyone can submit events
- Required event info link for verification

## Feature Ideas

### 1. Venue Directory
**Priority:** High
**Effort:** Medium

A searchable map/list of dart-friendly bars and venues in the area.

**Fields:**
- Venue name
- Address (with map pin)
- Number of boards
- Board type (Steel tip, Soft tip, Both)
- Food/drink menu info
- Hours of operation
- League nights hosted
- Open play availability
- Photos
- Member reviews/ratings

**Why:** Helps players find places to play, especially newcomers to the area.

---

### 2. Head-to-Head Rivalry Tracker
**Priority:** Medium
**Effort:** Medium

Track personal matchups between players over time.

**Features:**
- Overall record vs specific opponent
- Breakdown by game type (501, Cricket)
- Win streaks
- Notable matches (highest checkout against them, etc.)
- "Nemesis" and "Favorite Opponent" badges

**Example Display:**
```
You vs Mike Johnson
501:     3-7  (30%)
Cricket: 5-2  (71%)
Overall: 8-9  (47%)
Last Match: You won 2-1 on Jan 15
```

**Why:** Adds competitive fun, gives context before league matches.

---

### 3. Checkout Calculator
**Priority:** High
**Effort:** Low

Quick reference tool showing optimal checkout paths from any score.

**Features:**
- Enter score, see recommended path
- Show multiple options when available
- Indicate difficulty level
- "Bogey numbers" warning (scores with no 3-dart checkout)
- Could integrate with virtual darts game tips

**Why:** Extremely useful for newer players learning outs. Quick reference for everyone.

---

### 4. Practice Routines Library
**Priority:** Medium
**Effort:** Medium

Pre-built practice drills with instructions and score tracking.

**Drills to Include:**
- **Round the Clock** - Hit each number 1-20 in order
- **Round the Clock Doubles** - Same but doubles only
- **Bob's 27** - Start at 27, hit doubles to add points
- **100 Darts at T20** - Track how many treble 20s in 100 darts
- **Checkout Practice** - Random checkouts under 100
- **Bull Practice** - Hit X bulls as fast as possible
- **Cricket Marks** - Close all numbers solo, track rounds

**Features:**
- Instructions for each drill
- Score entry
- Personal best tracking
- Leaderboards (optional)
- Suggested routines by skill level

**Why:** Gives players structure for practice, tracks improvement.

---

### 5. Activity Feed / Social Wall
**Priority:** Medium
**Effort:** Medium

Central feed showing what's happening in the community.

**Feed Items:**
- Match results ("Team A beat Team B 5-4")
- Notable performances ("John hit a 180!")
- Tournament winners
- New member signups
- Marketplace sales
- Upcoming events
- Achievements earned
- League standings changes

**Features:**
- Filterable by type
- Like/react to items
- Comment on items (optional)
- Share to social media

**Why:** Creates community engagement, keeps people coming back to see what's happening.

---

### 6. Hall of Fame / Records
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** Low

Track all-time records and notable achievements.

**Records to Track:**
- Highest 3-dart average (single match)
- Highest 3-dart average (season)
- Most 180s in a match
- Most 180s in a season
- Fastest checkout (by darts)
- Highest checkout
- Longest win streak
- Most league championships
- Most tournament wins

**Clubs:**
- 180 Club (everyone who's hit one)
- 170 Club (hit the max checkout)
- 9-Darter Club (if anyone ever does it)

**Why:** Celebrates achievements, gives players goals to chase.

---

### 7. Player Availability Calendar
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** Medium

Let players mark when they're available for matches/events.

**Features:**
- Calendar view with availability slots
- "Looking for a game" status
- Integration with league scheduling
- Captains can see team availability
- Find pickup game partners

**Why:** Helps with scheduling, makes it easier to find games.

---

### 8. Equipment Reviews
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** Medium

Members rate and review their dart equipment.

**Categories:**
- Darts (barrels)
- Flights
- Shafts
- Dartboards
- Cases
- Accessories

**Review Fields:**
- Star rating (1-5)
- Pros/Cons
- How long owned
- Skill level of reviewer
- Photos
- Link to Dart Trader if selling

**Why:** Helps members make purchase decisions, adds value to marketplace.

---

### 9. League Draft Tool
**Priority:** Medium
**Effort:** High

Tool to help with league team drafts.

**Features:**
- Player pool with stats
- Draft order management
- Snake draft support
- Keeper/protection rules
- Live draft with turn timer
- Draft history/recap

**Why:** Makes draft night smoother, especially for larger leagues.

---

### 10. Push Notifications
**Priority:** High
**Effort:** Medium

Mobile push notifications for important events.

**Notification Types:**
- Match reminders (day before, hour before)
- Score updates for matches you're following
- Challenge requests
- New messages
- Tournament brackets posted
- League standings updates
- Marketplace interest in your listing

**Why:** Keeps users engaged, important info reaches them even when not on site.

---

### 11. Spectator Mode
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** High

Watch live matches remotely.

**Features:**
- Real-time score updates
- Throw-by-throw display
- Chat alongside match
- Multiple matches to choose from
- Replay completed matches

**Why:** Community engagement for those who can't attend in person.

---

### 12. Handicap Calculator
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** Low

Calculate fair handicaps for casual games between players of different skill levels.

**Features:**
- Enter both players' averages
- Suggest starting score differential
- Different formulas for 501 vs Cricket
- "What if" scenarios

**Why:** Makes casual games more competitive and fun.
